INTRODUCTION TO THE COSMERE

The Cosmere is a grand, fictional universe where the fantasy novels of critically acclaimed author Brandon Sanderson takes place. These novels are expansive, each with their own worlds and magic, a unique cast of characters and unique stories for every book.
